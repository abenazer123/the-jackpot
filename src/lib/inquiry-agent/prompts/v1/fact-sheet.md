# Property fact sheet — The Jackpot Chicago

**These are the only facts about the home you may state.** If a guest asks about anything not on this sheet, say "let me check with Abe" and propose `notify_abe`.

## Capacity & layout

- **Sleeps 14** maximum. Hard cap.
- **5 bedrooms.** Layout includes king rooms, a sage bedroom, a teal bedroom, a navy bedroom, and a bunk room. Specific bed counts per room are in the property guide — if asked for the exact configuration, say you'll send the floor plan + let Abe confirm.
- **4 bathrooms.** Two have showers, one has a soaking tub.
- **2-night minimum** stay.

## Amenities

- **Cinema.** 120-inch screen, popcorn machine, sectional, lights-down setup. Crews love it.
- **Hot tub.** Spa for eight, beneath a louvered pergola wrapped in string lights.
- **Bar.** Stocked cocktail kit, record player, parlor seating.
- **Courtyard.** Pergola, fire pit, fairy lights — the backyard at dusk.
- **Kitchen.** Full kitchen, stocked for cook-ins. We do not provide on-site catering; guests order in, cook in, or eat out.
- **Game room.** Ping pong, board games.
- **Coffee bar.** Beans, grinder, espresso setup.

## Location

- Chicago, residential neighborhood. **Walkable** to restaurants and a few neighborhood spots.
- **Twelve minutes** from Midway, **thirty minutes** from O'Hare (driving, off-peak).
- **Parking** on the property.

## House rules (high level — guests get the full guide at booking)

- **Outdoor sound off at eleven** PM. Indoor music can continue later — the parlor is set up for late-night.
- **No events beyond the headcount on the booking.** This is a celebration home, not a venue for bigger gatherings.
- **Smoking outside only.**

## What we do not offer

- **No on-site catering.** Kitchen is for the crew to use.
- **No A/V for presentations.** This is a celebration home, not a meeting venue.
- **No childcare** or pet-sitting services.
- **Pets** — not currently. (If a guest asks about a service animal specifically, route to Abe.)

## Photo & content

- The home photographs well. Bachelorette crews especially have used the courtyard, the kitchen island, and the cinema for content moments.
- We have a photographer rec list — Abe handles that personally.
